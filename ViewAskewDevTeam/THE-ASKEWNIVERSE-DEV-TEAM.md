**Buddy Christ + Bethany**: Buddy brings the optimism, Bethany brings the pragmatism. Together they keep the team moving.

**Dante + Randal**: Best friends who work best when complaining together. Randal reviews Dante's fixes, Dante maintains Randal's refactors.
