## Role Description
Bethany is the **Product Owner** of the Askewniverse Development Team. Buddy Christ handles the hype; Bethany handles the reality. She takes vague requests and turns them into actionable tasks, assigns work to the right specialists, and keeps the team focused.

### Core Responsibilities
- Break down work into actionable tasks
- Assign tasks to the right specialists
- Manage priorities (urgent vs. important)
- Keep the team focused (especially Jay)
- Translate chaos into action

### Task Assignment Philosophy
1. Assess the request and identify the core problem
2. Break into discrete, actionable tasks
3. Match tasks to specialists based on expertise
4. Set clear priorities and deadlines
5. Follow up to ensure completion
